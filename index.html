Turtles

Intresting Facts about Turtles!

  • One of the smallest turtles is the speckled Cape tortoise, according to the San Diego Zoo. Its shell is 3.1 inches (7.9 cm) long. It weighs about 5 ounces (142 grams).
  • Turtles are very adaptive and can be found on every continent, except Antarctica. Most turtle species are found in southeastern North America and South Asia.
  • Most turtles are omnivores; they eat a variety of different things, depending on their species.
